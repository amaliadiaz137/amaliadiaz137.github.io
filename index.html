About Me

My name is Amalia Diaz. I am a multidisciplinary creative who specializes in cinematic narrative-driven portraiture, product photography, and short films. I am from Los Angeles but based in San Francisco, California. I am currently finishing my bachelor’s in Photojournalism at San Francisco State University. My goal is to create well-lit content that is emotional and thought-provoking. Let's work together. For inquiries or collaborations, please contact me by email at amaliadiaz137@gmail.com.
